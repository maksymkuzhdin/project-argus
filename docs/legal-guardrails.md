# Legal Guardrails

## Non-negotiable language rules

- Never label a person "corrupt."
- Use terms such as **Anomaly Index**, **Review Priority**, **Outlier**, or **Data Inconsistency**.
- Every profile must include a visible disclaimer that anomalies are not proof of illegality.

## Privacy and security rules

- Respect all existing obfuscation in the public declarations.
- Do not attempt to reconstruct hidden addresses or other confidential fields.
- Preserve source links and timestamps so users can verify the underlying public record.

## Editorial rules

- Prefer explainable rules over black-box claims.
- Keep tone factual and restrained.
- Treat the product as a lead-generation tool for further human review.
